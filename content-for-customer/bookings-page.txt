BOOKINGS PAGE CONTENT
================================================================================

PAGE HEADER
-----------
Page Title:
Book a Trip

Page Subtitle:
Choose from our selection of guided fly fishing adventures


FILTER SECTION
--------------
[Note: The bookings page uses dynamic filters to help customers find trips. The filters include:]

Search Filter:
Search by trip name...

Difficulty Levels:
- Beginner
- Intermediate
- Advanced

Duration Options:
- Half Day
- Full Day
- Multi-Day

Location Options:
- Bow River
- Red Deer River
- Highwood River
- Oldman River
- Private Waters

Price Range:
Dynamic slider based on available trips


TRIP DISPLAY
------------
[Note: Individual trip cards are dynamically generated from the database. Each card displays:]

- Trip title
- Difficulty badge (color-coded: Beginner = green, Intermediate = amber, Advanced = red)
- Trip description
- Duration
- Maximum number of guests
- Price per person
- Trip photo (if available)
- "Book Now" button

When no trips match filters:
Heading: No trips match your filters
Description: Try adjusting your filter criteria to see more results.
Button: Clear Filters

When no trips are available at all:
Heading: No trips are currently available
Description: Please check back soon or contact us directly for availability.


TRIP DETAILS (when clicking on a trip card)
--------------------------------------------
[Note: Opens a modal with full trip details including:]
- Full description
- Location details
- What's included
- What to bring
- Difficulty level information
- Price breakdown


BOOKING FORM (when clicking "Book Now")
----------------------------------------
[Note: Opens a modal with a booking form that collects:]
- Customer name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Preferred date
- Number of guests
- Special requests or questions
