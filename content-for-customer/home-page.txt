Signature Alberta Guided Trips

Section Description:
Choose from Bow River drift boat adventures, Red Deer River sight-fishing, or foothills walk-and-wade journeys. Each guided fly fishing trip includes transportation on the water, premium rods and reels, hand-tied flies, and expert instruction.

Trip 1: Bow River Drift Boat Trips
Location: Bow River - Calgary & Southern Alberta
Description: Full-day guided float trips on Alberta's legendary Bow River targeting trophy brown and rainbow trout with hoppers, streamers, and nymph rigs.
Highlights:

Trip 2: Red Deer River Sight-Fishing
Location: Red Deer River - Badlands Region
Description: Technical walk-and-wade or raft-supported days stalking aggressive browns in sight-fishing runs through the central Alberta badlands.
Highlights:

Location: Highwood / Oldman / Livingstone Rivers
Description: Immersive Alberta Rockies experiences exploring clear freestone streams with patient instruction for dry-fly, Euro-nymph, and tight-line tactics.

- Custom itineraries for single or multi-day packages
