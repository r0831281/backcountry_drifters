Licensed Alberta Guide Team

Section Heading:
Meet Our Alberta Fly Fishing Guides

Bio Paragraphs:

Paragraph 1:
With over 15 years of professional guiding across the Bow River, Red Deer River, and southern Alberta foothills, Backcountry Drifters delivers the region's most intentional guided fly fishing experiences. Our small team is fully licensed, insured, and singularly focused on creating safe, conservation-forward adventures.

Paragraph 2:
Born and raised in Calgary, our guides refined their craft rowing the Bow River, stalking the Red Deer badlands, and hiking into the Highwood and Oldman systems. That on-the-water time translates into precise reads on hatches, flows, and trout behavior so every outing doubles as an immersive classroom.

Paragraph 3:
Whether you are casting for the first time or refining advanced streamer presentations, our coaches pair patient instruction with premium gear, seamless logistics, and an unwavering commitment to Alberta fisheries. We are here to design the guided trip that matches your goals.


GUIDED TRIP SPECIALTIES SECTION
--------------------------------
Section Heading:
Guided Trip Specialties

Section Description:
Explore the Alberta fly fishing packages we run most often. Each service below can be customized for solo anglers, small groups, or corporate retreats, and includes premium rods, reels, flies, river lunch, transportation on the water, and conservation education.

Specialty 1: Bow River Drift Boat Trips
Description: Iconic full-day floats covering 15-25 kilometers of blue-ribbon water minutes from downtown Calgary with steady brown and rainbow trout action.
Location: Bow River, Calgary and Southern Alberta
Season: Prime season April-October

Specialty 2: Red Deer River Walk-and-Wade
Description: Sight-fishing missions through the Red Deer badlands targeting aggressive browns with terrestrials, streamers, and technical dry flies.
Location: Red Deer River corridor
Season: Best mid-June through September

Specialty 3: Foothills Freestone Adventures
Description: Custom itineraries on the Highwood, Oldman, and Livingstone Rivers that blend alpine hikes with patient instruction for dry fly and Euro nymph tactics.
Location: Southern Alberta foothills
Season: Late May through early October

Specialty 4: Multi-Day Rockies Expeditions
Description: Fully planned Alberta fly fishing packages combining drift boat days, walk-and-wade exploration, camp catering, and conservation-focused education.
Location: Central and Southern Alberta service area
Season: Available May-October

OUR WATERS SECTION
------------------
Section Heading:
Our Waters

Section Introduction:
Backcountry Drifters Fly Fishing specializes in the premier fly fishing waters of the Alberta foothills and Calgary area. Our primary fishing grounds include:

Water 1: Bow River
World-renowned for its blue-ribbon trout fishing, the Bow offers exceptional year-round opportunities for brown and rainbow trout.

Water 2: Highwood River
A pristine foothill stream known for its scenic beauty and excellent dry fly fishing.

Water 3: Oldman River
Remote sections offering solitude and challenging conditions for experienced anglers.

Water 4: Private Waters
Access to exclusive spring creeks and private ranch waters for the ultimate fly fishing experience.

Closing Statement:
Each location is carefully chosen based on seasonal conditions, water levels, and hatches to ensure the best possible fishing experience.
